1) Commas (,) in the values
In the .csv files we can find some entries (values) presenting commas (,).
In the specific case of these datasets, the commas simply represent a division (for readability) of a whole number.
The cleaning performed on the commas is simply removing them from the entry and leaving the whole number as is.

2) Broken characters in country and cities names
The broken characters have been fixed

3) % symbol in percentage values
The percentage (%) symbol has been removed

4) $ symbol in monetary values
the $ symbol has been removed
